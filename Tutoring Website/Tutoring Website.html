    Who We Are

    CLTutoring is a student-led club, consisting of dedicated highschoolers who are passionate about making a positive impact on our community. Our mission is to provide free tutoring services to students in grades 10 and below, by offering academic support and guidance to help with their studies. Whether you need help preparing for an exam, studying for a subject, or need general advice, we are here for you!
